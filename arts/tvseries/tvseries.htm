Preferred Citation: Collection of Television Series Scripts (Collection 81). UCLA Library Special Collections, University of California, Los Angeles.

Scope and content: The collection consists of American television series scripts. The bulk of the collection dates from the ca. 1960’s to the present. The collection includes television series scripts of mixed genres and aired by various television networks including ABC, CBS, NBC, TNT, WB, Fox and more. Includes some scripts annotated by the writers.
