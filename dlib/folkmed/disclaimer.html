The intent of the Online Archive of American Folk Medicine is to provide comparative and historical information about traditional medical beliefs.  The data provided here is for educational purposes only.  The University of California, Los Angeles and the project personnel do not make any claims as to the effectiveness of folk or alternative medical therapies, nor do they officially endorse the use of alternative therapies over conventional medicine.  Always consult with a qualified health practitioner before deciding on any course of treatment, especially for serious or life-threatening illnesses.  None of the information contained in the database should be considered as professional medical advice.
