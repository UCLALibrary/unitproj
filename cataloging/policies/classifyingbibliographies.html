Classifying Bibliographies

April 11, 2003

Subject bibliographies falling into Z5000+

For all locations including branches, classify subject-related bibliographies with other materials on the same subject. This will make for a more usable, browsable collection in the long term. Apply this only to newly-cataloged materials.

Exception: Serial title changes, involving continuity of a single numbering system across multiple records, should have the same call number on the complete run of volumes.

Bibliographies of literary authors falling into Z8000+

Classify bibliographies of literary authors with the authors' works, i.e. using the P schedules and their instructions for assigning cutters. Bibliography is considered Biography and criticism.

Bibliographies of literary authors that were previously classified in Z will be reclassified at the request of bibliographers and reference librarians. New and reclassified bibliographies will receive LC-style cutters for authors, including those that have established local cutters. We will not reclassify the works of a literary author for the purpose of changing the local UCLA cutter to the LC cutter.
