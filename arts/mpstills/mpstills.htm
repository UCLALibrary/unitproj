Preferred Citation: Collection of Motion Picture Stills (Collection 195). UCLA Library Special Collections, University of California, Los Angeles.

Scope and content: This collection is primarily American motion picture stills dating from ca. 1920s to the present. The collection includes black and white prints and a small amount of color prints and slides. The photographs include set and scene shots as well as publicity stills. The collection represents films produced by various companies including Columbia Pictures, 20th Century Fox, MGM, Paramount, Republic, RKO Universal, United Artists, Warner Brothers and more.
